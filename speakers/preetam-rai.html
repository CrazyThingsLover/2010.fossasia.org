FOSS in Education/ Android Apps

Preetam Rai is a technologist involved in training educators. He travels extensively and helps put up technology and culture events. He has been active in cross-connecting regional IT communities in Asia. His talk will look at the use of open source software in education and try to list the resources that are available to train teachers in using them. He will also talk about the open and user generated events happening across East and South East Asia.
